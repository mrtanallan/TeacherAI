Common questions

The stuff teachers ask before signing up.

Is my student data safe? +

Yes. TeacherAI runs on Supabase Canada Central — your data stays in Canada. Student names are never sent to the AI; only anonymized accommodation notes (e.g. "ELL Level 2") are used for personalization. Designed with Ontario's MFIPPA in mind from day one.

Is using AI for lesson planning actually okay? +

Your professional judgement is what makes a lesson work. TeacherAI doesn't replace it; it gives you a starting draft instead of a blank page. Every lesson, assessment, and comment is yours to review, edit, or rewrite before it reaches a student or parent. The AI drafts. You decide.

What if I don't like what it generates? +

Edit it. Every lesson, worksheet, rubric, and comment is fully editable in your browser. Tweak the wording, swap questions, fix anything. Worksheets have a "More Questions" button if you need extras, and report card comments can be regenerated per student. You're never stuck with what the AI gave you on the first try.

Does it really work for split grades? +

Yes. This is one of the things TeacherAI was built for. Pick multiple grades (1/2, 4/5, 6/7) and you get separate worksheets per grade with grade-appropriate expectations for each. The lesson plan handles the differentiation, and the grade tracker records each student's level against their own grade's expectations once you've approved it. Works for any split grade configuration from Grades 1 through 8.

Will it work for Kindergarten? +

Yes. TeacherAI supports the new Ontario Kindergarten Curriculum (2026) with play-based "Play Invitation" lesson plans, K-specific worksheets with images, and observation-based assessment instead of Level 1-4 rubrics. Activities are designed for print-and-write. Kids draw, trace, and circle on paper while you observe.
